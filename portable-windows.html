SRWare Iron Portable for Windows

Run Iron without installation. Ideal for locked‑down PCs, shared machines, or launching from a USB drive.

Download installer (64‑bit) Install Guide More download options

Note: Portable editions may be offered by the vendor. If unavailable, use the standard installer or check the vendor’s site for alternative packages.

What “portable” means

No system install

Launch Iron directly from a folder or USB drive without writing to system locations.

Self‑contained profile

Your browsing data stays in the portable folder, making backups and moves simple.

Limited integration

Portable apps don’t register as default handlers by default and may not auto‑update.

When to use a portable build

Great for

  • Shared or locked‑down PCs without install rights
  • Testing a browser without touching system settings
  • Carrying your profile between machines via USB

Keep in mind

  • May not auto‑update like installed apps
  • Less OS integration (default browser, protocols)
  • Performance depends on your USB drive speed

Managing your portable profile

Backup easily

Copy the portable folder to create a snapshot of your extensions, bookmarks, and settings.

Stay safe

Protect your USB drive with encryption if it stores sensitive data. Avoid public PCs you don’t trust.

Update strategy

When a new version is released, replace the app files while keeping your portable profile folder intact.

For maximum privacy, combine portable use with recommended privacy settings and extensions.

Portable FAQ

Is a portable build officially available?
Portable editions may be provided by the vendor. Availability can vary by version. If you can’t find one, the standard installer remains a safe choice.
Will portable Iron auto‑update?
Often no. Check for new releases and refresh the app files manually while keeping your profile.
Does portable keep my data separate?
Yes, that’s the goal of portable use: to keep your data within a self‑contained folder you control.
